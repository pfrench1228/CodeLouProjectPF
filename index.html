    At Hill View Farms Market, we sell meat, produce, and Kentucky products made just for you. Our farm fresh beef is grown right in your community by our family, the Gilles'. We also support our other local farming families here in Western Kentucky and bring to you our own selection of Kentucky Proud products! Thank you for supporting us.
    Hill View Farms is the modern day family business of the 5th generation Gilles family here in Owensboro, Kentucky. Our family has supported our local community since the 19th century, and we call this place home. We want to provide you access to our heritage, values, and family life through this store. You can visit us at Hill View Farms Market or at the weekly Farmers Market. Thank you for shopping local!
